Future Authors: Artificial Intelligence or Human?
Artificial intelligence (AI)
Artificial intelligence's ability to produce content leads to interesting discussions about how the writing profession will evolve in the future. Artificial intelligence takes over routine and time-consuming writing tasks, offering writers the opportunity to focus on more creative work. However, the limits of AI's creativity are linked to its ability to fully reflect human emotions and experiences. Therefore, a future in which artificial intelligence and human writers work together to complement each other's shortcomings seems possible.

The Mysterious World of Artificial Intelligence
FIRST AND LAST AI
Artificial intelligence is a branch of science that enables computers to think, learn and solve problems like humans. So, how is this possible? Thanks to techniques such as machine learning and deep learning, artificial intelligence learns from large data sets and develops capabilities that can mimic human intelligence. In this way, artificial intelligence is now able to write content text on its own.
Year 2100
Machine Learning
What Can We Do? What Can We Discover
Machine learning is a branch of artificial intelligence that allows computers to learn and make predictions by analyzing data. In this field where statistical techniques are used, algorithms extract patterns and relationships from large data sets, thus learning. Machine learning has a wide range of applications, from spam email filtering to product recommendation systems.
Year 2100
Deep Learning
What Can We Do? What Can We Discover
Deep learning is a subset of machine learning that uses artificial neural networks inspired by the neural networks of the human brain to process data. This method is particularly powerful at recognizing and understanding complex patterns on large data sets. It has revolutionized fields such as image and voice recognition and language translation.
Year 2100
Natural Language Processing (NLP)
Image Processing
Natural language processing is a technology that enables computers to understand and process human language data. Advances in this field have made many applications possible, from voice assistants to automatic translation services. NLP can perform complex tasks such as text summarization, sentiment analysis, named entity recognition. Image processing is a field in which digital processing techniques are used to extract information from images. When integrated with artificial intelligence, this technology can be used in many areas such as facial recognition, medical image analysis, and vehicle license plate recognition. Deep learning models have significantly increased the accuracy and efficiency of image processing.
